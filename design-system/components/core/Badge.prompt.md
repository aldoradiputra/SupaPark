**Badge** — small tinted label for status or counts.

```jsx
<Badge variant="success">Paid</Badge>
<Badge variant="warning">Pending</Badge>
<Badge variant="error">3</Badge>
<Badge variant="info">Override</Badge>
<Badge variant="outline">Closed</Badge>
```

Variants: `default` · `success` · `warning` · `error` · `info` · `outline`. Fills are status-color at 15% opacity. For lane/gate/payment/member values with the right label + dot, prefer `StatusBadge`.
